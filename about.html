WHO ARE WE
The Tema Fuel Company LTD is a privately owned petrochemical tank farm and loading gantry facility located in the Tema Heavy Industrial area on the Valco Road. The terminal boasts of a 80,000 MT capacity storage for Diesel [AGO] and Premium Motor Spirits [PMS] as well as 4,000 MT capacity tanks for storing Liquified Petroleum Gas [LPG] as well as gantry loading facilities for the above mentioned fuels and is fed via the CBM pipeline to serve all licensed Bulk Distribution Companies in Ghana. Our highly automated Terminal offers the reliable receipt, storage and distribution of our customers products in a safe and secure environment.
